Quads Glutes Compound

Barbell Back Squat

Sets

4

Reps

8-10

Tempo

3-1-1

list_alt Execution Steps

1

Position the barbell across your upper traps, not your neck. Grip the bar firmly with elbows tucked back.

2

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly pointed outward. Inhale deeply and brace your core.

3

Lower your hips back and down until your thighs are parallel to the floor, keeping your chest proud.

4

Drive through your mid-foot to return to the starting position, exhaling at the top of the movement.

lightbulb

Coach Zack's Insights

"Focus on vertical bar path. If you feel your heels lifting, your weight is too far forward. Think about 'screwing' your feet into the floor to activate your glutes more effectively."
